Three Unique Store Models

E

Emporium (Indoor)

Built on computer vision, deep learning, and multi-sensor fusion technologies, we can accurately identify product information and customer shopping behaviors, provide “grab-and-go” frictionless shopping experience to customers, and help stores to reduce labor cost and increase efficiency.

These stores provide a seamless shopping experience, allowing customers to enter, select products, and exit without the need for traditional checkouts or cashiers.
O

Oasis (Outdoor)

Extend your retail presence to remote areas effortlessly with On The Way's innovative remote store solution that is suitable for the outdoors. Powered by cutting-edge technologies, including computer vision, deep learning, and multi-sensor fusion, our solution is designed to bring modern retail convenience to underserved locations.
